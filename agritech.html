Proudly South African and Committed to Contributing to Our Local Economy

We’re not just an aquaponics farm; we’re a vision in action. Our mission is to revolutionize how South Africans eat, offering a path to a healthier, more sustainable future.

Since our humble beginnings in a small, rented premises in the Vaal, DCBC-SA has grown into a major pillar of the community. We are deeply committed to supporting all customers, from structural steel and engineering to the agricultural sector. Our success stems from our understanding of industry needs and our dedication to responding to these needs.

Aquaponics Training Courses

We aim to offer Aquaponics Training Courses every 2-3 months, depending on the season. These courses cover the basics of hydroponics and aquaculture, providing essential knowledge for beginners and experts alike.

Our training workshops are primarily held near Lanseria, Johannesburg, where we operate a commercial aquaponics system. Attendees gain invaluable insights, often saving far more than the cost of the workshop itself.
