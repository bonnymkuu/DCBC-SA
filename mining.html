What is Urban Mining?

Urban mining involves extracting valuable materials from waste, including common metals, plastics, and rarer elements. It salvages finite resources, reduces environmental impact, and avoids further extraction from ecosystems.

At DCBC-SA, we process old laptops, phones, and more to recover materials like steel, leveraging advanced mining technology and science.
